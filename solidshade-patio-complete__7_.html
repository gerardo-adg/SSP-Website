Our Story

About Solid Shade

Solid Shade Patio was founded on one simple belief: Sacramento homeowners deserve better than cookie-cutter backyards built by crews that disappear after the deposit clears.

Owner Alex Vladymtsev started in the trades over 18 years ago and built this company from the ground up — one backyard at a time. Every project we take on is handled personally. You'll have Alex's direct number, and he stays involved from first walkthrough to final sign-off.

We serve Citrus Heights, Sacramento, Roseville, and the surrounding region. We're not a national franchise, not a call center that dispatches strangers — we're your local crew, and we stand behind everything we build.
